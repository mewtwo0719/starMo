Our Story

A Rustic Soul
by the River's Edge

Nestled in the cobbled streets of Mostar's old town, just a stone's throw from the iconic Stari Most, StarMo is more than a restaurant — it's a gathering place where generations have shared stories, laughter, and the honest food of the Balkans.

We source our ingredients daily from local farms in the Herzegovina hills, and our recipes have been passed down with love — slowly, stubbornly, the way good food should be.
